#### The Prologue of the 2026 NorCal Hike and Fly Contest will run from March 1st to March 31st! 
[Register here](https://forms.gle/Jpu2PvBmsTkQpAfY6) to join the prologue.


####  NorCal Hike and Fly Contest aims to bring folks together for friendly competition in the sport of hike and fly.  


<div style="margin-left: 40px;">

Pilots submit and are scored on hike and fly tracklogs.
At the end of the year, each pilot's top 4 scores are combined to give their season score.  
Winners will be announced in EN-B, EN-C and EN-D classes.  
Prizes to be determined. Beer is likely to be involved.
</div>


Pilots are incentivized through scoring to join up and fly with others, but no formal events are planned.
