### Tracklog Recording

**Please do not use the SportsTrackLive app to record tracklogs!**

#### Android — [XCTrack](https://xctrack.org) (Recommended)

**XCTrack setup for Hike and Fly Tracks:**
* Go to Preferences menu
* Find Automatic Actions
* Set the following:
    * Landing Detection: No Detection
    * Takeoff Speed: 1 km/h

#### iOS
Options: 
* SeeYou Navigator (Great hike and fly mode)
* Gaia (may require subscription)

#### Dedicated Vario / Flight Computer

Most varios and flight computers record IGC tracklogs natively. Some (Naviter) support Hike and Fly tracklogs. Export the IGC file via USB or the manufacturer's companion app after your flight day.

#### Dedicated GPS Tracker, Smartwatch, Etc.

GPX tracks are allowed, so you can record your day's flight with any device which generates a timestamped GPX tracklog file with GPS position. 

#### Tips

- Remember to record for the entire day. Start recording before you leave your car and stop after you return. The scoring system will truncate tracks to the 6am–6pm window automatically.
- All scoring is done from a single continuous tracklog. If you record the hike and fly portions separately, you can use [this tool](https://merge.norcalhf.com) to merge them before submitting a combined track.
- Submit within **7 days** via the Tracklog Upload tab.
